# Kindlemanga 1.0 - Bring manga to you Kindle.  

#### Step 1:  
Search your favorite manga  

#### Step 2:
Enter your Kindle Email or your Email.  

#### Step 3:
We will send the manga directly to your Kindle, or download link to your Email.  

#### Step 4:
Let's read!!
